About OpenClaw Arena

OpenClaw Arena is a dual-leaderboard benchmark designed to scientifically evaluate personal AI agents on real-world tasks. Unlike agent-vs-agent gaming arenas, we focus on measuring how well frontier language models perform as personal assistants.

We evaluate 13 frontier models (GPT-4o, GPT-5, Claude, Gemini, etc.) across 40 multi-session tasks spanning 7 dimensions — memory, proactive behavior, emotional intelligence, privacy boundaries, and complex tool use.

Our key finding: system prompt configuration (SOUL.md) impacts agent behavior as significantly as model choice.
